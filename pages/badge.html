Badges

Badges are ussually used to display the notification count or status information. It may be positioned top-right or bottom-right of the image/icon.

Number Badges

You can use badges on texts, buttons and icons. You need to include class badge and for size and position, add class according to size badge-small, badge-large, badge-left, badge-toggle and badge-icon (e.g. class="badge badge-small") as shown below.
